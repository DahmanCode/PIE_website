Session 3 : Your mission as training entrepreneurs.

An entrepreneur is a person who encounters problems along the way and whose mission is to resolve them in order to succeed.

About Session

Objective :

  • Identify a new definition of entrepreneurship.
  • Show trainees a new perspective on their problems.
  • Apply a positive and creative approach.
  • Learn to take responsibility.

Learnings :

The entrepreneur, like the creative person, always starts with a blank sheet. He has a dream, an idea and he is going to turn it into reality.

Home works :

Identify problems encountered in daily life.
